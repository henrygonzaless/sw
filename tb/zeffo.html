Zeffo Unlock Mission

Mods Setup (Recommended)

  1. Cere Junda: Health (143-248 Speed)
  2. Jedi Knight Cal Kestis: Health, Speed (255-303 Speed)

Operational Parameters

  • Switch between stances as much as possible to maintain tactical advantage.

Engagement Strategy

  1. Wave 1: Focus damage/armor shred on one enemy and utilize Cal's instakill on the other.
  2. Wave 2: Execute immediate instakill on the Inquisitor, then consolidate fire on the remaining target.
