Third Sister Mission Guide

Mods Setup (Recommended)

  1. 7th Sister: Health, Speed (317-353)
  2. 8th Brother: Crit Dmg/Chance (293-307)
  3. 5th Brother: Health, Speed (303)
  4. Grand Inquisitor: Health, Offense (276-288)
  5. 9th Sister: Defense (231-254)

General Tips

  • NO AOE abilities unless you have Tenacity Up (achieved via 6 Purge stacks + GI's 3rd special).
  • Save GI's cleanse (middle ability) for when allies have 6 or more dots.
  • Save 5th Brother's stun for Wave 2 unless critical.

Turn Sequence

  1. 7th Sister: Apply Daze on Chief Nebit.
  2. 5th Brother: Basic attack on Nebit to stack Purge.
  3. GI: Once Nebit has 6 Purge stacks, use right-most ability for Tenacity Up. Otherwise, Basic.
  4. 8th & 9th Sister: If Tenacity Up is active, use AOE and target Jawa Scavenger. Otherwise, Basic Nebit.
  5. Once Nebit is controlled and Scavenger is down, clean up remaining targets.
