Mods Setup (recommended)

  1. 7th Sister (Health, Speed; 317-353)
  2. 8th Brother (Crit Dmg/Chance; 293-307)
  3. 5th Brother (Health, Speed; 303)
  4. Grand Inquisitor (Health, Offense; 276-288)
  5. 9th Sister (Defense; 231-254)

Things to Remember

  • No AOE abilities unless you have tenacity up. This is achieved with 6 stacks of purge then using GI's right-most ability.
  • Save GI's cleanse (middle ability) for when your inquisitors have 6 or more dots.
  • Save 5th Brother's stun for wave 2 unless absolutely needed.
  • Use 7th Sister's heal on the lowest ally on her 2nd turn.

General Strategy

  1. 7th Sister: Daze on Nebit
  2. 5th Brother: Basic on Nebit for purge
  3. GI: If Nebit has 6 stacks of purge, use the right-most ability (tenacity up). Otherwise, just basic attack on Nebit to stack purge.
  4. 8th Brother: If you have tenacity up, use the aoe and target Scavenger. Otherwise, just basic attack on Nebit to stack purge.
  5. 9th Sister: If you have tenacity up, use the aoe and target Scavenger. Otherwise, just basic attack on Nebit to stack purge
  6. Once Nebit has 6 stacks and you have tenacity up, use all aoe and get Scavenger first.
